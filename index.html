The Legend of the Ledger

Deep within the school's oldest library, legends tell of the Headmaster's Ledger—a book containing not rules, but the secrets to a perfect education. Its wisdom is sealed behind five linguistic trials, each testing a teacher's knowledge of school life.

Now, the new school year has begun. As Lyra, a dedicated teacher, you seek the ledger to inspire your students. But you are not alone. Four rival teachers are on your trail, each wanting the ledger for their own reasons. The race is on!
